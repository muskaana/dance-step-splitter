How it works

  1. 1

    Add a video

    Paste a YouTube URL and click Process video, or click Upload & process local video to use an MP4 from your computer.

  2. 2

    Crop the choreography

    Skip the intro / outro: scrub to where the dance actually starts, hit Use playhead → Start, and do the same for the end. Then click Process Video in the crop panel.

  3. 3

    Edit segments

    The auto-detected segments appear on the right. Click Edit to tweak start/end times, split at the playhead, add or delete a segment. Save when happy — your edits go to your library.

  4. 4

    Pick your settings

    Below the video: change Speed (slo-mo for tricky moves), toggle Mirror Mode to follow along like a class, and switch On Audio / On Counts to hear a 1–8 count instead of music.

  5. 5

    Practice

    Click individual segment buttons to loop them, combine several to drill a run, or hit Start Workshop for a guided chunking-and-chaining session: drill 1, drill 2, combine 1+2, drill 3, combine 1+2+3, and so on.
